About the Five Pillars

The Five Pillars of Digital Health is an original framework by Ufuoma Okpeahior describing digital health as five interdependent stages: Foundation (infrastructure), Lifeblood (data), Compass (leadership & strategy), Bedside (clinical practice & informatics) and Future (education & training).

Every vacancy here is classified against that framework by an open, versioned taxonomy. Read the framework →

Coverage & known limitations

Listings are drawn from the public NHS Jobs search (jobs.nhs.uk) using a fixed set of digital health search terms, refreshed monthly. This will not capture every digital vacancy: roles advertised only on other platforms, titled in unusual ways, or posted and closed between refreshes may be missed.

Classification is keyword-based and imperfect by design — roles that can’t be confidently assigned to a pillar are excluded rather than guessed. Always confirm details on the original advert, which each role links to.

Independent project. The Five Pillars Digital Health Vacancy Tracker is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced in partnership with the NHS, NHS England, NHS Business Services Authority, or any NHS organisation. All vacancy data is drawn from publicly available job listings; each role links to its original advert on the official NHS Jobs service, where applications are made.
